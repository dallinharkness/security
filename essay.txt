The Community:

	The community is a group of hackers from around the world. Five members of the group just pleaded guilty to SIM swapping. It is a form of identity theft where a person persuades a phone carrier into giving a victims phone number to their SIM cards. In doing so the group of hackers can then use peoples phone numbers to steal peoples personal information. In the case, the hackers used the phone numbers to steal millions of dollars worth of cryptocurrency. the article does not provide more information on the securtiy threat, however if mentions to keep your personal information very private.
